FactNews

This app allows users to improve their media literacy by taking a critical eye towards what news items are forwarded to them in a quick, informative dashboard. This “one-stop shop” will automate cross-checking for quick verification and provide a more complex approach of machine learning as an option, so they can understand why and how to verify digital information.
